Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?

You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.

2. Is this website free to use?

Yes, our website offers a wide range of free resources. Some advanced courses and features may require a subscription, but the core content is always accessible at no cost.

3. Do I need to create an account?

You can access most of the content without an account. However, creating a free account allows you to track your progress, save your favorite lessons, and participate in community forums.

4. How can I build my English vocabulary?

We have dedicated sections for vocabulary building, including flashcards, word games, and thematic word lists. Regular practice and using new words in sentences are key.

5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?

Yes, upon successful completion of our certified courses, you will receive a digital certificate that you can share on your professional profiles.
